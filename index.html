Common questions
Are the documents legally valid in South Africa?
RentReady generates documents with reference to the Rental Housing Act 50 of 1999 and other applicable South African legislation. They are designed to be comprehensive starting points. For complex or disputed matters, we recommend consulting a qualified attorney.
Which provinces are supported?
Currently Western Cape, Gauteng, KwaZulu-Natal, and Eastern Cape. Documents reference the correct provincial Rental Housing Tribunal in each case.
Can I customise the documents?
Yes — every document includes a special conditions field where you can add specific terms, and the generated text can be edited before saving or sending.
How do I cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account settings. No lock-in, no cancellation fees.
