Common questions
Are the documents legally valid in South Africa?
RentReady generates documents with reference to the Rental Housing Act 50 of 1999 and other applicable South African legislation. They are designed to be comprehensive starting points. For complex or disputed matters, we recommend consulting a qualified attorney.
Which provinces are supported?
Currently Western Cape, Gauteng, KwaZulu-Natal, and Eastern Cape. Documents reference the correct provincial Rental Housing Tribunal in each case.
What is the clause library?
Pro users get access to a library of optional pre-written clauses covering pets, utilities, parking, security, and more. Toggle them on and they drop straight into your lease. You can also describe any custom clause in plain language and Claude writes it for you.
Is Lite right for me?
If you own one property and sign one lease a year, Lite is perfect. At R199/year you get 10 clean documents with no watermark and PDF download — everything a single-property landlord needs.
Do I need to sign up to try it?
No signup needed for the free tier. Just click a document and start. You only need an account when you upgrade to Lite or Pro.
How do I cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account settings. No lock-in, no cancellation fees.
